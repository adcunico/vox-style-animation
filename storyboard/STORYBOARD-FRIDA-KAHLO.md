## Master Script

> A bus crash shattered her spine at 18. A steel handrail impaled her body. Doctors said she might never walk again. So Frida Kahlo picked up a paintbrush — in bed.
>
> While the art world chased abstraction, Frida painted herself — 55 times. Not vanity. Surgery, miscarriage, heartbreak — she put her pain on canvas when no one else would dare.
>
> Critics called her a surrealist. She shot back: "I paint my own reality." Today, Frida isn't just Mexico's most famous artist — she's proof that your wounds can become your voice.
